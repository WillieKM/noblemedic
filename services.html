Medical Transportation Services

Noble Medic Wheels provides professional non-emergency medical transportation services designed to safely transport patients to healthcare facilities, medical appointments, dialysis treatments and hospital discharge locations.

♿ Wheelchair Transportation

Our wheelchair accessible vehicles are designed to provide safe and comfortable transportation for patients who require wheelchair support when traveling to medical appointments.

🚶 Ambulatory Transportation

Ambulatory transportation services are available for patients who can walk independently but require dependable transportation to medical appointments and treatments.

🩺 Dialysis Transportation

We provide scheduled transportation services for dialysis patients who require recurring transportation to treatment centers several times per week.

🏥 Hospital Discharge Transportation

Our hospital discharge transportation service ensures patients are safely transported from hospitals or medical facilities to their home, rehabilitation center or assisted living facility.
