Our Transportation Services

Noble Medic Wheels provides professional non-emergency medical transportation services for patients requiring safe, comfortable, and reliable travel to healthcare facilities.

Wheelchair Transportation

Specialized wheelchair-accessible vehicles equipped with ramps and secure restraints to ensure safe and comfortable transportation for mobility-impaired passengers.

Ambulatory Transportation

Transportation for passengers who can walk independently but require assistance traveling to medical appointments or healthcare facilities.

Dialysis Transportation

Reliable recurring transportation for dialysis patients needing scheduled rides to and from treatment centers.

Hospital Discharge Transportation

Safe transportation for patients leaving hospitals, rehabilitation centers, or surgery facilities who require assistance getting home.

Medical Appointment Transportation

Reliable rides to doctor's appointments, physical therapy, specialist visits, and outpatient procedures.

Senior Transportation

Compassionate transportation services for seniors living independently, in assisted living communities, or in long-term care facilities.
