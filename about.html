About Noble Medic Wheels

Noble Medic Wheels provides dependable non-emergency medical transportation for patients across King County, Pierce County, and Snohomish County. Our focus is providing safe, comfortable, and reliable transportation for individuals who require assistance getting to medical appointments.

Transportation should never be a barrier to receiving healthcare. Our team is committed to helping patients arrive at their appointments on time and with the support they need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every patient has access to reliable transportation for essential healthcare services. We believe that dependable transportation plays a critical role in maintaining health and improving quality of life.
