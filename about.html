Who We Are

Noble Medic Wheels is a trusted provider of non-emergency medical transportation services for patients who require dependable transportation to healthcare facilities. We specialize in transporting passengers safely to medical appointments, dialysis treatments, hospital visits, and rehabilitation centers.

Our goal is to ensure every passenger experiences professional service, comfortable travel, and reliable scheduling for important healthcare needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver dependable medical transportation services that prioritize patient safety, comfort, and punctual arrival for healthcare appointments. We are committed to helping patients access the medical care they need by providing reliable transportation throughout Western Washington.
