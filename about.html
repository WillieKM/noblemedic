About Noble Medic Wheels

Noble Medic Wheels provides dependable non-emergency medical transportation services designed to help patients travel safely to medical appointments, dialysis treatments, hospital discharge locations and healthcare facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, safe and comfortable transportation services for patients who require dependable medical transportation to healthcare providers.

We are committed to helping patients reach their appointments safely while providing healthcare facilities with a dependable transportation partner.

Professional Drivers

Our drivers are trained to provide professional, courteous and reliable transportation services while ensuring the safety and comfort of every passenger.

  • Professional and courteous drivers
  • Patient-focused transportation service
  • Reliable scheduling and pickup times

Safe & Comfortable Vehicles

Our transportation vehicles are maintained to high safety standards and designed to provide comfortable transportation for patients traveling to healthcare facilities.

  • Wheelchair accessible vehicles
  • Clean and well-maintained transportation vans
  • Comfortable patient seating

Service Area

Noble Medic Wheels proudly serves patients and healthcare facilities throughout the region including:

  • King County
  • Snohomish County
  • Pierce County
  • Seattle
  • Auburn
  • Tacoma
  • Kent
