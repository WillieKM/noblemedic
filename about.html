About Noble Medic Wheels

Noble Medic Wheels provides dependable non-emergency medical transportation for patients throughout King County, Pierce County, and Snohomish County. We focus on safe, comfortable, and on-time transportation for individuals who require assistance traveling to medical appointments.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every patient has access to reliable transportation for essential healthcare services. We believe transportation should never be a barrier to receiving care. Our drivers are trained to assist passengers with compassion, professionalism, and respect.
