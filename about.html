About Noble Medic Wheels

Reliable non-emergency medical transportation serving communities across King County, Pierce County, and Snohomish County.

Who We Are

Noble Medic Wheels provides dependable non-emergency medical transportation for patients across King County, Pierce County, and Snohomish County. Our mission is to ensure safe, comfortable, and reliable transportation for individuals who require assistance traveling to medical appointments.

Transportation should never be a barrier to receiving healthcare. Our team is committed to helping patients arrive at appointments on time and with the support they need.
